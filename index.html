Al Ahly’s Duo Loaned Out to FK Teplice & MFK Zemplín Michalovce

Amir Tawfik, head of the club’s transfers department, announced that Al Ahly’s youngsters: Mido Hossam and Ahmed Biso have been loaned out to FK Teplice of the Czech Republic and MFK Zemplín Michalovce of Slovakia, respectively. Al Ahly's loan system policy aims to give our youth players the opportunity to gain new experiences abroad and promote them to the best leagues in Europe.

El Khatib Heads Al Ahly Media Production Company

Al Ahly's board of directors, headed by president Mahmoud El Khatib, held a meeting on Saturday at the club's premises in Gezira to discuss several important files. Following the meeting, it was decided that president El Khatib will be assigned as the chairman of Al Ahly Media Production Company.

24-hour Rest for Al Ahly Players

Pitso Mosimane, Al Ahly's football first team head coach, decided to give his players a 24-hour rest on Monday. Our players will be back in action on Tuesday to start their preparations for Morocco's Raja CA in the first leg of the CAF Champions League quarter-finals.

Mosimane: The Youngsters Are the Future of the Club

Pitso Mosimane, Al Ahly’s head coach, attended the U-16 team’s game against El Entag El Harby on Monday at the club’s grounds in Nasr City. Mosimane was impressed by the performance of the team during the game, which ended with a 4-1 win for the young eagles. Our first-team coach met the team after the game and told them that he is very happy with their performance. “I am so happy to be here with you today and I wish to see you in the future with the first team. You are the future of the club.”

Handball: A Defeat at Cairo Derby

Al Ahly men's handball team lost to Zamalek 34-28 on Friday night in the Egyptian League. Dr Hassan Mostafa Hall saw a strong performance from the two teams and ended in a victory for our opponents.

Six Al Ahly Youngsters Join Youth National Team

Al Ahly players: Mostafa Makhlouf, Mohamed Ahmed, Eslam Abdullah, Youssef Sherif, Raafat Khalil and Mido Nabil joined the U-20 Egyptian national team camp on Tuesday. The camp is being held between 28 December and 3 January as part of the preparations for the upcoming U-20 Africa Cup of Nations in Egypt in 2023.

El Khatib Thanks the UAE for the Great Hospitality

Mahmoud El Khatib, Al Ahly’s president, thanked the UAE for the great hospitality that our delegation enjoyed during the 2021 FIFA Club World Cup and praised the team’s efforts in winning the bronze medal of the FIFA Club World Cup. .

Al Ahly Start Preparations for Raja Game

Al Ahly players gathered today at El Tetsh Stadium to start their preparations for Raja CA of Morocco game in the CAF Champions League. The players, who participated in Al Masry's game, carried out several recovery exercises at the gym. The rest of the players performed fitness drills under Kabelo Rangoaga, the team's fitness coach, before participating in a strong training game. The Red Eagles are set to face Raja CA in the first leg of the CAF Champions League.

Volleyball: Al Ahly to face Zamalek in the Cairo Derby

Al Ahly men’s volleyball team will face Zamalek on Sunday at 8:00 PM in the Egyptian League at Suez Canal Authority Hall. The “Masters” will be looking forward to sealing an important win in the Cairo derby in order to maintain their winning streak in the competition.

Mortagy Meets Al Wahda FC’s CEO

Khaled Mortagy, the club’s board member and treasurer, met Khaled Al Hanaei, Al Wahda FC’s CEO, after the end of the team’s pre-match training session in Abu Dhabi on Friday. During his short meeting with Mortagy, Al Hanaei was keen to gift the head of Al Ahly’s delegation in Abu Dhabi with Al Wahda FC’s jersey..

Starting Line-up Announced for Al Masry’s Game

Pitso Mosimane announced the team’s starting line-up for our game against Al Masry in the Egyptian Premier League.

Basketball: Al Ahly Defeat Zamalek to Reach Cup Final

Al Ahly men's basketball team booked their place in the Egyptian Cup final with a big win over Zamalek, 77-67, in the semi-final on Saturday night at Dr Hassan Mostafa Hall. The Red Eagles won the game by 10 points after a dominant performance from our players, who had the upper hand from the beginning of the game.
